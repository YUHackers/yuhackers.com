Welcome to MTA Hackers!

A high school club about building interesting things with programming, creativity, and teamwork.

About

MTA Hackers is a community of YUHSB high school students interested in learning and building things like apps, websites, and hardware projects. Open to every student, regardless of skill level.


MTA Hackers are doers, artists, programmers, designers, and engineers.

Events

Hacker Hours

Weekly meetups with a coding, design, or hardware workshop, anyone can come with their laptop, hangout, code, and ask a mentor for help.

Reinvent:YU

Yeshiva University's 24 hour Hackathon happening after Shabbos on May 7th. Signup at http://reinventyu.com and contact yuhackathon@gmail.com for questions.
